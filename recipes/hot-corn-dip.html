Hot Corn Dip

Description

This hot corn dip uses cream cheese, canned corn, and jalepenos that goes great with salty chips!

Ingredients

  • Canned Corn
  • Cream Cheese
  • Jalepenos

Directions

  1. Empty the corn into the crockpot
  2. Chop up the jalepenos and add them to the corn
  3. Add cream cheese to the crockpot
  4. Serve when melted together
