Spinach and Artichoke Dip

Description

This spinach dip adds in artichokes and sauces for a great flavor and party addition

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Artichokes
  • Mayo
  • Sour Cream
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Garlic

Directions

  1. Add frozen spinach to a crockpot
  2. Chop garlic and drained artichokes and add them to spinach
  3. Add half cups of mayo and sour cream to crockpot
  4. Serve when melted together
