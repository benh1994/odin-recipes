Cheese Dip

Description

This cheese dip takes velveeta, burger, and rotel tomatoes to make a great app for parties!

Ingredients

  • Velveeta Cheese
  • Burger
  • Rotel Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Brown the burger and add taco seasoning
  2. Chop up velveeta cheese into cubes and add to crockpot
  3. After adding burger to crockpot, top with rotel tomatoes
  4. Serve when melted together
